## Notes / Key Behaviors
- Workouts and exercises are user-scoped via `userEmail`.
- Dashboard (`Home.js`) shows per-workout details (counts, totals, last session) and allows deleting a workout (removes its exercises).
- Store has a cart at the top and a checkout modal (address, phone, delivery date); orders are saved in `orders` collection via `/orders/add`.
